# Nobretech Marketing Growth
You are working on the Nobretech Store ERP/CRM.
This skill applies whenever the task involves:
- product promotion
- commercial copywriting
- Instagram stories
- reels scripts
- feed captions
- Meta Ads
- WhatsApp sales messages
- OLX ads
- customer reactivation
- campaign planning
- offer structures
- proof of trust
- objection handling
- lead conversion
- marketing automation
- Nobretech brand positioning
- product launch communication
- campaign ROI context
- marketing modules inside the ERP
The goal is not to generate generic copy.
The goal is to help Nobretech sell more with trustworthy, premium, direct, human and commercially intelligent communication.
## Brand Context
Nobretech Store sells Apple products and technology devices, mainly:
- iPhones
- iPads
- MacBooks
- Apple Watch
- accessories
- bundles/kits
- sealed products
- used/seminovo products with condition proof
Nobretech does not communicate like a generic electronics store.
The brand positioning is:
- premium
- transparent
- confident
- close to the customer
- practical
- direct
- trustworthy
- “partner to partner”
- high perceived value
- not pushy
- not fake luxury
- not desperate
Nobretech should sound like a real person who understands technology and knows how to guide the customer to buy safely.
## Voice and Tone
The Nobretech commercial tone should be:
- human
- natural
- persuasive
- clear
- calm
- confident
- consultative
- premium without arrogance
- direct without sounding rude
- friendly without being childish
- urgent without sounding desperate
Avoid:
- generic AI tone
- exaggerated hype
- fake scarcity
- robotic sales language
- too many emojis
- corporate filler
- empty phrases
- overpromising
- manipulative pressure
- “imperdível” repeated everywhere
- sounding like a dropshipping ad
- sounding like a cheap bazaar
Nobretech can use urgency, but it must be grounded:
Good:
- “Tenho poucas unidades nessa condição.”
- “Essa condição depende da disponibilidade da cor.”
- “Enquanto esse lote estiver disponível.”
- “Hoje ainda consigo te entregar com esse kit.”
Bad:
- “Última chance da sua vida.”
- “Corre antes que acabe!!!”
- “Promoção surreal.”
- “Oferta inacreditável.”

Every output should help the customer understand:
1. What is being offered.
2. Why it is valuable.
3. Why it is trustworthy.
4. What is included.
5. What risk is reduced.
6. What action to take next.
A good Nobretech offer usually combines:
- product
- condition
- price
- warranty
- included items
- delivery/local availability
- payment options
- trust proof
- limited availability
- clear CTA

2. Sale/campaign data

4. Warranty/condition data

Offer Anatomy

A strong offer should usually include:

* product name
* storage/capacity, if relevant
* color/variation, if relevant
* condition: sealed, seminovo, grade A, intact, etc.
* warranty: Apple warranty or Nobretech warranty
* price
* parceling conditions
* included gifts
* delivery/location
* trust proof
* CTA

For Apple products, condition matters.

For used products, include:

* battery health, if available
* cosmetic condition
* warranty period
* box/accessories, if available
* transparency/procedência
* proof on delivery

For sealed products, include:

* sealed
* original
* warranty validation
* activation/serial check when applicable
* kit included, if any

Story Structure

Stories should not be random slides.

Use structure.

Default 5-story structure:

1. Hook
2. Desire or problem
3. Offer
4. Trust/proof
5. CTA

Example:

Story 1 — Hook
Tu ainda está procurando um iPad completo para estudar, trabalhar ou presentear?
Story 2 — Desire
O problema é que muita gente compra o iPad e depois ainda precisa gastar com caneta, capa e película.
Story 3 — Offer
Aqui na Nobretech ele já sai com kit completo: iPad 11 lacrado + caneta + capa + película.
Story 4 — Trust
Produto original, lacrado e com garantia Apple.
Story 5 — CTA
Me chama agora que eu te passo as condições e vejo a disponibilidade da cor.

Stories should be short.

Do not overload each story with text.

One idea per story.

Reels Scripts

Reels should sound like a real person talking.

Default 30-second structure:

0-3s: hook
3-10s: context/problem
10-20s: offer/value
20-27s: trust/proof
27-30s: CTA

Keep teleprompter scripts short.

Avoid formal writing.

Use spoken Portuguese.

Example opening styles:

Cara, se tu estava esperando uma condição boa pra pegar um iPad, olha isso aqui.
Tu não precisa comprar o iPad e depois sair gastando com acessório separado.
Esse aqui é pra quem quer comprar certo, sem dor de cabeça.

WhatsApp Copy

WhatsApp messages should be:

* concise
* personal
* clear
* easy to reply to
* not too long
* not overly formatted

Use WhatsApp markdown when useful:

*Produto*
_De R$ X por R$ Y_

Do not overuse emojis.

Good CTA:

Quer que eu veja a disponibilidade dessa cor pra você?
Posso te mandar as condições de parcelamento?
Se fizer sentido pra você, já deixo separado.

Avoid:

Compre agora!!!
Promoção imperdível, aproveite já!!!

Meta Ads Copy

Meta Ads needs clarity fast.

Every ad variation should have:

* primary text
* headline
* description, if useful
* CTA suggestion

Good ad copy should quickly answer:

* What is it?
* What is included?
* Why buy now?
* Why trust Nobretech?

Avoid too much text in creative.

For ad copy, create variations by angle:

1. Value/bundle
2. Trust/warranty
3. Urgency/limited stock
4. Gift/present
5. Productivity/study/work
6. Premium opportunity

Do not make unsupported claims like:

* “menor preço do Brasil”
* “melhor da cidade”
* “garantia total”
* “sem risco”
* “aprovado pela Apple”

unless explicitly supported.

Objection Handling

Common objections:

* “Onde fica a loja?”
* “Tem garantia?”
* “Tem nota?”
* “É original?”
* “Faz mais barato?”
* “Por que está esse preço?”
* “Posso ver antes?”
* “Entrega onde?”
* “Parcela em quantas vezes?”

Responses should be:

* honest
* confident
* not defensive
* transparent
* grounded in actual policy

Never lie about:

* store location
* invoice
* warranty type
* Apple warranty
* product origin
* device condition
* battery health
* sealed status

Trust Language

Nobretech should build trust through specifics, not vague promises.

Use:

Você pode conferir serial/ativação comigo na hora.
O aparelho é entregue com garantia Nobretech de 6 meses.
Produto lacrado, original e com validação pela própria Apple.
No portal da compra, você acompanha os dados do pedido e garantia.

Avoid:

100% garantido
sem risco nenhum
confia
produto top

Discount and Gift Framing

When a product includes gifts, show perceived value.

Example:

iPad 11 lacrado
+ caneta
+ capa
+ película
Você já sai com o kit completo.

If showing gifts as discount:

De R$ 80,00 por R$ 0,00
Brinde incluso nessa condição

Do not hide real price mechanics.

If a color costs more or has lower margin, explain clearly:

A condição da cor prata está melhor porque o custo dela ficou menor nesse lote.
Na rosa eu consigo manter a caneta e a película como brinde, mas a capa eu consigo fazer num valor especial.

Product Availability

If a product is sold, copy should use it as proof, not frustration.

Example:

O 15 Pro Max já foi vendido, mas ainda tenho duas opções muito boas disponíveis hoje.

If a color is limited:

Nessa condição, a disponibilidade muda por cor.

The Central de Divulgação should feel:

* premium
* commercial
* focused
* fast to use

* not cluttered
* not a plain textarea generator

Recommended layout:

* left panel: product and strategy inputs
* right panel: generated content cards

* generic AI chat box
* purple gradient SaaS look

AI Behavior In The Module

AI should:

* write human copy
* use facts from ERP
* ask for missing commercial data
* suggest angles
* generate multiple versions
* adapt to channel
* avoid unsupported claims
* flag weak offers
* suggest better positioning
* warn if margin/stock does not support the offer

AI should not:

* invent prices
* invent warranty
* invent scarcity
* invent gifts
* invent payment conditions
* modify stock
* modify price
* create campaign spend without approval
* claim performance without data

Integration With Business Rules

The marketing module must respect:

* stock availability
* reserved/sold/returned/repair status
* real price
* warranty type
* gift/upsell rules
* payment terms
* customer-facing transparency
* product condition
* supplier/stock constraints when relevant

If a product is sold or unavailable, copy should not promote it as available.

If product has limited quantity, copy may mention limited availability.

If a price depends on color/variation, copy must reflect that.

Conversion Quality Checklist

Before final output, check:

* Is the offer clear?
* Is the product identifiable?
* Is the value obvious?
* Is the trust argument specific?
* Is the CTA easy to answer?
* Is the copy human?
* Is it too generic?
* Is it making unsupported claims?
* Is the channel format respected?
* Is there a stronger angle?
